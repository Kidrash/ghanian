Ghanaian Akan Names

We all have names, but not every one knows how, or what criteria was used for them to get their names. However there are some people who know where their names origianated from. These are people from Ghana, a country in west Africa. Ghanaians have a naming system where they get named depending on the day of the week in which they are born.

*HOW IT WORKS*

By providing your details as required below the application will take your birth dates and calculate the day of week in which you were born hence giving you your Akan name.
